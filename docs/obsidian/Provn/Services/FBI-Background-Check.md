# FBI Background Check

## Overview

Identity history summary (rap sheet) obtained through electronic fingerprint submission to FBI CJIS. Required for immigration, visa applications, international adoption, overseas employment, professional licensing, and foreign residency.

## What's Included

- Electronic fingerprint capture at our office
- Submission to FBI CJIS
- Identity history summary report

## Common Use Cases

- **Immigration & visa applications** — most countries require FBI clearance
- **International adoption** — Hague Convention requirement
- **Overseas employment** — background clearance for foreign employers
- **Professional licensing** — healthcare, legal, education licenses
- **Foreign residency** — permanent residency applications abroad
- **Study abroad** — some programs require criminal background check
- **Military/government clearance** — security clearance applications

## Process

1. Client schedules fingerprinting appointment
2. Fingerprints captured electronically at our office
3. Submission to FBI CJIS
4. Results received (timeline varies)
5. If apostille needed → order transitions to apostille workflow

# Fingerprinting Services

## Overview

Professional ink fingerprint cards for clients who need physical fingerprint cards for various licensing, employment, and legal requirements.

## Key Details

- **Price:** $99 per card
- **Location:** In-office only — 2125 Biscayne Blvd Suite 303, Miami, FL 33137
- **Processing time:** ~20 minutes
- **Format:** Ink cards (can be sent as PDF — not electronically)

## What This Service Is For

Fingerprint cards are used for:
- **Out-of-state submissions** — licensing boards in other states requiring physical cards
- **Concealed weapons permits** — required for concealed carry license applications
- **Security guard licenses** — state-mandated background screening
- **FBI background checks** — fingerprints submitted to FBI CJIS
- **Healthcare licensing (DOH)** — nurses, doctors, home health aides
- **Real estate licensing (DBPR)** — Florida real estate agents
- **Childcare (DCF)** — daycare workers, foster parents
- **Immigration (USCIS)** — visa and green card applications
- **Adoption** — domestic and international adoption requirements
- **Banking & finance** — FINRA, NMLS mortgage licensing
- **TSA PreCheck / Global Entry** — trusted traveler programs
- **Bar admission** — attorney licensing
- **Volunteer organizations** — schools, youth programs

## Important Notes

- Cards are **ink-based** (professional quality)
- Cards can be **sent as PDF** to requesting agencies — not submitted electronically
- Client must bring valid government-issued photo ID to appointment
- Walk-ins accepted, but appointments recommended
